Working Together

📑
By Project

Needs change from project to project. You may need discovery research that could take a week or two, or not. Or, requirements might change after engineering teams plan their release, requiring significant design revisions.

This is good for companies that need consistent help for a project with a beginning and an end.

⏱
Fixed Time Units

Choose from one day, one week, one month, or one quarter. With fixed time units, there is no guarentee of a project's completion.

This is good for companies or organizations needing temporary help or on a tighter budget.

🗓
Monthly Retainers

❤️ Best Value

A rolling engagement meant for long-term professional relationships. Choose from 5, 10, 15, or 20 hours per week.

This is good for companies needing consistent help throughout the year on a variety of projects.

🔍
Critique

A 1-hour video chat where we'll review of any project that you're working on. You'll receive structured and actionable feedback on interface, interaction, and visual design, messaging, branding, typography, and more.

This is good for companies that want an outside perspective on your work, or for junior designers needing guidance.
