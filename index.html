About Us

Science & Design is operated by Glenn Sorrentino. Glenn is a product designer and user researcher that's spent over a decade designing systems and building products at scale for a leading Fortune 500. His work has led to US patents, invitations to collaborate with organizations and institutions like New America, Harvard, and the World Economic Forum, and has helped to set industry standards for interface and interaction design.

After providing pro-bono services to digital rights non-profits and open-source projects for nearly the same amount of time, he's focusing full-time on helping to build software in that's in the public interest.
